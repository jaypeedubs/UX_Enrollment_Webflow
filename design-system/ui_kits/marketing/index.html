01 Cochlear Implant Training

The Institute for Cochlear Implant Training

Online, certificate-bearing courses for surgeons, audiologists, and their multidisciplinary teams — built around weekly live web classes, asynchronous didactics, and hands-on clinical practica.

Founded 2018 Miami, FL In Memory of Dr. Thomas Balkany
01 Memorial Tribute

In memory of Thomas J. Balkany, MD

A pioneer of cochlear implantation and lifelong educator. The Institute carries forward his commitment to clinical training, mentorship, and evidence-based care.

"To restore hearing is to restore connection — to family, to language, to the life one knows."

— Dr. Thomas Balkany, 1944–2023

02 Curriculum

Six tracks for the multidisciplinary CI team

Each course is taught online with live weekly web classes, recorded didactic lectures, knowledge assessments, and a moderated discussion board.

ASC
Advanced Surgeons

Advanced Cochlear Implant Surgeons

Subspecialty training in CI surgery for infants, children, and adults — adds depth to neurotology and pediatric otolaryngology fellowships.

13 weeks · Hands-on lab
AAC
Advanced Audiology

Advanced Cochlear Implant Audiology

Focused on programming nuance, troubleshooting, and complex candidacy — for audiologists already practicing CI.

12 weeks · Live + async
FAC
Foundational Audiology

Foundational Cochlear Implant Audiology

A first deep training in CI audiology — candidacy, device selection, surgical implications, and initial programming.

10 weeks · Online
ISC
International Surgeons

International CI Surgeons Course

A surgical curriculum tailored for international practice patterns and device availability.

12 weeks · English
IFAC
International Foundational

International Foundational Audiology

Foundational programming and candidacy training for audiologists practicing outside North America.

10 weeks · Online
CITEC
Team Efficiency

CI Team Efficiency Course

Workflow, scheduling, and care-pathway training for the full CI clinical team — schedulers, surgeons, audiologists, SLPs.

8 weeks · Self-paced
03 MSTB v3

Minimum Speech Test Battery

Updated and streamlined test battery for pre-operative determination of candidacy and post-operative assessment of cochlear implant performance in adults.

  • v3.1Test files, scoresheets, and recommended quiet/noise levels
  • PDFManual covering protocol, stimulus calibration, and reporting
  • XLSXEditable scoresheet templates for clinic workflows
  • FREEDistributed at no cost to qualified clinics — sign-up required
