03Mechanisms

Three distinct insertion injuries

Each pathway is independently modifiable — surgical technique addresses all three when applied together.

  • A.Direct trauma — basilar membrane disruption from electrode contact during advancement.
  • B.Hydraulic trauma — perilymph wave generated by the array, transmitted to apical structures.
  • C.Inflammatory cascade — fibrotic and ossific response over weeks following insertion.
