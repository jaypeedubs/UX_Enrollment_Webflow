Application Portal · Button Variants (Webflow classes)
Primary — navy (next / continue)

Webflow class: btn-primary-1-2 or btn-primary-1. Use for forward progression only.

Ghost — transparent (back / save draft)

Webflow class: btn-ghost-1. Secondary or reversible actions.

Submit — green (final submission)

Webflow class: btn-submit. Only on the final submission step.

Danger — red outline (destructive actions)

Webflow class: btn-danger-1 or btn-danger. Use for any action that permanently removes or cancels something — Withdraw, Remove CV, delete. Always red-outlined, never red-filled. The hover state adds a faint red tint (#fef2f2) to signal danger without aggression.
