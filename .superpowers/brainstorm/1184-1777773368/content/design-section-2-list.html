Hover → ··· submenu
Ellipsis button appears on row hover (right-aligned). Menu: Open, Accept, Waitlist, Request More Info, then Reject (red, separated by divider). Available actions adjust per status — e.g., a waitlisted row removes "Waitlist" and shows "Accept".
